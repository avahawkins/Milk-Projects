Recipe Three: Flavoured Milk

This milk project is both fun and tasty! Using this basic recipe, you can customize it endlessly to create homemade fresh flavoured milk that has both amazing flavour and less sugar.

Tip: Try blended with different fruits to create new flavours!

You Will Need:

  • A Blender, Fork or Whisk
  • A Sweetener (maple syrup, honey or agave)
  • 1-2 cups of milk
  • Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract

Note: This is vanilla milk, the base for creating more flavours

Optional (Some Require a Blender)

  • 1/2 cup of Ripe Banana for Banana Milk
  • 1/2 cup of Strawberries for Strawberry Milk
  • 1 teaspoon of Cocoa Powder for Chocolate Milk

Instructions:

  1. Add all the ingredients to either a cup or blender
  2. Mix until all ingredients are blended together
  3. Pour into glass and enjoy!
